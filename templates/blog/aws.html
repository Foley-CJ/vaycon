NoSQL Databases Analysis: Twitter Utilization


NoSQL databases have been gaining popularity ever since they first appeared in 1998. Since that point, multiple offerings have entered the space each with a slightly different value proposition. Given the uniqueness of each offering, it can be difficult to detemine which product to deploy. One dimension of that comparision that's more simple to evaluate is the level of support and community surrounding the offering.

In this analysis we investigate that specific dimension by evaluating how each the utlization of Twitter by three of those offerings: MongoDb, DynamoDb, and Casandra. in 2021 by Twitter API , Tweepy, Plotly, and Sentiment Analysis?
